Why did I take BSIT?

Here's Why!

In my opinion, everyone has an obligation to study. We should learn so that we can make a positive contribution to our country. IT courses were a "boom" in a period when nursing was also on the rise; nevertheless, I did not chose this degree since I am not interested in programming or computers, it just happened that my parents wanted me to pursue this degree. I will recommend this course to other students because computers are used in almost every job nowadays. Whatever you choose to do after graduation, or even if you want to change careers, I can assure you that what you'll learn in IT will be useful.
